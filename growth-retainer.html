Why a retainer wins

Ads become a channel, not an experiment, when someone is iterating on them every week. A retainer lowers your effective per-asset cost because the research and brand learning is amortised across everything you ship, and it means winners get scaled and losers get killed fast instead of a beautiful asset being delivered and forgotten.

This is how we built Shivora Ghee's entire ad system from a launch SKU to a premium FMCG presence, and how our creative makes up roughly 70% of SuperMoney's public Meta Ad Library. See how the math works in the pricing guide, or view current rates.

[ 02 ] FAQ

Common questions.

What is included in the retainer?
A monthly volume of tested creative plus the strategy around it: a rolling test plan and iteration on winners. The Creative + Performance track adds Meta and Google Ads, audience strategy, and weekly reporting.
What is the difference between the two tracks?
Creative-only means we deliver assets and your team runs media. Creative + Performance means we own both content and media, tied to revenue, as one loop.
When does a retainer beat per-video pricing?
If you plan to test more than 6 to 8 creatives a month, a retainer almost always wins on cost-per-winning-ad.
