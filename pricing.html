[ 02 ] Retainer

When ads become
a channel.

A monthly retainer buys a volume of creative plus the strategy around it: testing plans, iteration on winners, and optional media management. It lowers your effective per-asset cost because the research is amortised across everything you ship.

Creative only

We deliver a monthly volume of tested creatives; your team or agency runs the media. Includes research, a rolling test plan, and iteration on what performs. Priced to your volume.

Creative + Performance

We own both content and media: Meta and Google Ads setup, audience strategy, weekly reporting, and continuous creative iteration tied to revenue. One team, one loop.

Rule of thumb: if you plan to test more than 6 to 8 creatives a month, a retainer beats per-video pricing on cost-per-winning-ad.

[ 03 ] Pricing FAQ

Straight answers.

What drives the price of an AI ad?
Four things: research depth, iteration count, character and brand consistency, and finishing (sound design, pacing, captioning, per-placement versions). The tools are commoditised, so you are paying for the thinking, not the software.
How should I budget my first project?
Budget for a test batch of 4 to 6 creatives (roughly ₹25,000 to ₹60,000 depending on craft level), run them with a modest media spend, and let the data pick the winner. Once you have one, shift to a retainer and iterate on it.
Do you also run the ads, or just make them?
Both, your choice. Creative-only means we deliver the assets and your team runs media. Creative + Performance means we own Meta and Google Ads setup, audience strategy, reporting, and iteration.
What should I not pay for?
Endless revisions on a bad concept, per-hour billing on generative work, and volume with no research behind it. You want a studio testing new angles and owning the result, not polishing a loser.
